Somewhat Spicy Chili

Description

This is an easy crockpot turkey chili recipe that you can quickly throw together in the slow cooker and forget about all day. Great topped with Cheddar cheese, crushed corn chips, and a dollop of sour cream.

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound of Ground Turkey
  • 1 medium white onion
  • 2 cans of kidney beans
  • 1 can of black beans
  • 2 10.75 oz cans of tomato soup
  • 1 pack of spicy taco seasoning
  • Jalapenos, garlic, salt, and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Line a crockpot with cooking spray
  2. Lightly cook ground turkey in a skillet
  3. Combine all ingredients in a crockpot
  4. Cook on high for 4 hours, cook on low for 2 hours
